Cielo Payment Acquirer

Cielo Payment Gateway by CandidRoot Solutions introduces a new 'Payment Acquirer' method, allowing configuration of 'Cielo Merchant ID' and 'Cielo Merchant Key' for secure transactions, similar to other payment acquirers like Stripe, PayUmoney, Paypal, etc.
